About Us

ChatSupport

History:
 This is the Official Portal of the Government of India, designed, developed and hosted by the Team Yantrix as part of the Samsung Innovation Campus Hackathon Programme. The goal was to provide a comprehensive and efficient support system that could offer instant solutions to all user queries and concerns.
Objective / Vision:
The objective behind this website is to provide reliable and accurate information to users and help them with their questions and problems. The vision is to create a support system that can cater to the needs of all users and offer them the best possible assistance.
Portal Management:
The chat support on this website is managed by the members of Team Yantrix who developed it under the Samsung Innovation Campus Hackathon Programme. The team manages the content of the website and ensures that all user queries are answered promptly and accurately. The aim is to continuously enhance and improve the support system to offer users the best possible experience.
